Tableau Project: Analyzing Airbnb Rental Listings

  • Project Dashboard Page

Project Description:

This project showcases my ability to leverage Tableau for data visualization and analysis. I explored a comprehensive Airbnb rental listings dataset to gain insights into rental property characteristics, pricing trends, and geographical distributions.

1. Visualizations: The interactive dashboard provides a variety of visualizations to explore the data, including:

  • Map: A geographical heatmap illustrates the distribution of rental listings across different locations.
  • Bar Charts: Bar charts reveal trends in listing prices based on factors like zipcode and number of bedrooms.
  • Line Charts: Line charts depict how average listing prices vary by week of date.

    • 2. Key Insights: By interacting with the dashboard, users can uncover valuable insights such as:

  • Popularity of Different Locations: Identify areas with a high concentration of Airbnb listings, potentially indicating tourist hotspots or areas with high demand for short-term rentals.
  • Room Configuration and Price: Explore how the number of bedrooms and amenities offered influence rental prices.
  • Impact of Time on Pricing: Observe how pricing varies based on week of the year, allowing for informed decision-making for both hosts and renters.

    • This project demonstrates my proficiency in data source connection and manipulation within Tableau, constructing various chart types (bar charts, line charts, maps) to effectively represent data, and creating interactive dashboards for data exploration and storytelling.
